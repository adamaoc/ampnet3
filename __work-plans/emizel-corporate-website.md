## **Project Overview**

**Client:** Emizel  
**Project Type:** Corporate / Brochure Site  
**Infrastructure Model:** Developer-Owned Infrastructure

### **Project Description**

A professional corporate website for Emizel featuring a modern design with 5 sub-pages, integrated contact management through FlexHub, and featured links system. The site will be hosted and maintained by AmpNet Media with full email management setup.

## **Technical Stack & Infrastructure**

### **Development Stack**

- **Framework:** Next.js 15 with App Router (Static Export)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4
- **Language:** TypeScript with React 19
- **Contact Management:** FlexHub API integration
- **Email Service:** Zoho Business Email
- **Hosting:** DigitalOcean App Platform
- **Domain Management:** AmpNet Media managed

### **Infrastructure Details**

- **Hosting:** DigitalOcean App Platform ($10/month)
- **Image Hosting:** DigitalOcean Spaces ($5/month)
- **Email Hosting:** Zoho Business ($3/month)
- **Email Sending:** Resend API ($5/month)
- **Domain Registration:** $2/month
- **FlexHub CMS:** $5/month
- **Total Monthly Infrastructure:** $30/month

## **Project Scope & Deliverables**

### **Core Pages**

1. **Homepage** - Hero section, featured links from FlexHub, company overview
2. **About** - Company story, mission, values
3. **Services** - Detailed service offerings with descriptions
4. **Portfolio/Work** - Showcase of completed projects
5. **Contact** - Contact form integrated with FlexHub, contact information
6. **Blog/News** - Content management through FlexHub (optional)

### **Key Features**

- Responsive design (mobile-first approach)
- FlexHub API integration for contact form submissions
- Featured links system pulling from FlexHub
- SEO optimization
- Contact form with email notifications
- Professional corporate design
- Fast loading and performance optimized
