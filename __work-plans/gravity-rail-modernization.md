## **Questions for Client Meeting**

### **Technical Questions**

1. **Current WordPress Setup**

   - What WordPress version is currently running?
   - What theme and plugins are being used?
   - Do you have access to WordPress admin dashboard?
   - Are there any custom post types or custom fields?
   - What plugins are essential to preserve functionality?

2. **Domain & DNS Management**
   - Who currently manages the domain registration?
   - Do you have access to DNS settings?
   - Are there any existing email accounts or services tied to the domain?

### **Design & Branding Questions**

3. **Logo Update**

   - Do you have a new logo design ready, or do you need one created?
   - If creating new logo, what style direction do you prefer?
   - Are there any brand guidelines or color preferences?

4. **Design Preferences**
   - Do you have examples of websites you admire or want to emulate?
   - Any specific colors, fonts, or visual elements you want to include?
   - Any elements from the current site you want to keep?

### **Content & Functionality Questions**

5. **Content Updates**

   - Do you have new content ready for the updated services?
   - Are there any additional pages or sections you want to add?
   - Do you need help with content writing or copywriting?

6. **Current Website Functionality**
   - Are there any current features or functionality that must be preserved?
   - Any contact forms, integrations, or special features currently working?
   - Do you have Google Analytics or other tracking already set up?

### **Business & Timeline Questions**

7. **Project Timeline**

   - Is there a specific deadline for the project?
   - Do you need the site to remain live during development?
   - Any specific launch date or business requirements?

8. **Ongoing Management**
   - Do you want us to manage hosting and maintenance going forward?
   - Who will be responsible for content updates after launch?
   - Do you need training on how to update the site?

## **Risk Assessment**

### **Low Risk Factors**

- ✅ Clear project scope and requirements
- ✅ Modern technology stack
- ✅ Well-defined deliverables

### **Medium Risk Factors**

- ⚠️ Unknown current codebase complexity
- ⚠️ Content delivery timeline
- ⚠️ Design approval process

### **High Risk Factors**

- ⚠️ **Current hosting/infrastructure unknown** - Could impact migration complexity
- ⚠️ **Existing functionality dependencies** - May require preservation of current features

### **Mitigation Strategies**

- **Technical Discovery First** - Thorough analysis of current setup before pricing
- **Phased Approach** - Design approval before development begins
- **Content Timeline** - Clear content delivery schedule and requirements
- **Backup Strategy** - Full backup of current site before any changes
